Island of Siquijor

Siquijor is an island province located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. It lies southeast of Negros, south of Cebu and Bohol, and southwest of Dumaguete (Negros Oriental). It is surrounded by the Bohol Sea and is known for its beautiful beaches, waterfalls, and mystical folklore.

When is the best time to visit Siquijor?

November to April are the driest months in Siquijor. Due to the tropical climate though you can still experience sudden showers here even when it’s not rainy season.
